Ice Cream History

Timeline of Ice Cream

200 BC

First ice cream-like dessert created in China using milk and rice

1295

Marco Polo brings ice cream recipes from China to Italy

1744

First ice cream recipe published in America

1851

First commercial ice cream factory opens in Baltimore

1904

Ice cream cone invented at the St. Louis World's Fair
